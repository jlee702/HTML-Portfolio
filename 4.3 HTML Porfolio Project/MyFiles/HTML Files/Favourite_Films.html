My Favourite Movie/Show List

as of 2023

By Joel Lee

Film 1:

Cyberpunk: Edge Runners

A very solid animation that is held in the Cyberpunk world

A little dark, and has a very good ending :)

Film 2:

The Harry Potter Series

All of the movies are very nostalgic and always fun to rewatch after a few years

The first 3 are a little childish, but it definitely picks up in the latter half

Film 3:

Studio Ghibli Movies

I love the earlier ones: Spirited Away, Castle in the Sky, Ponyo, Princess Monomoke etc.

Always a classic and very entertaining to watch.
